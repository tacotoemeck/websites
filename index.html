About us

Maria Sabina started as a small market stall in Brixton back in 2013. It's a product of passion for everything Mexican. It's our mission to bring you the best of Mexican flavours. We don't like it easy. Everything we make takes time and effort. That's the thing with Mexican food. Monica learnt that from the generations of cooks back home in Guanajuato and brought their skills and passion to the street of London.

Our food is always fresh and made with a lot of love. We are particulary proud of our tortillas. They are made by hand ( with a help of a automated press and cooking chasis). We make them fresh so you can enojoy the best of Mexican flavours here at our Soutbank stall every weekend.

Over the past few years we served our delicious tacos and quesadillas in places such as Pop Brixton, Roof East, various festivals and now Southbank Centre Food Court.
